The reason to visit

A shoe fitting should feel personal, precise, and fast.

01

Listen first

Start with goals, training, injury history, preferred surfaces, and what has or has not worked before.

02

Measure both feet

Size, width, arch profile, and fit preferences guide the first round of options.

03

Watch movement

Staff observe stride, comfort, and stability so the recommendation is rooted in how you actually move.

04

Compare shoes

Try several models with real feedback, not guesswork from an endless wall of boxes.

05

Dial in comfort

Socks, inserts, lacing, and small fit details turn a good choice into the right choice.

06

Leave ready

Walk out with shoes and gear matched to your next mile.
